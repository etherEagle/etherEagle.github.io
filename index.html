Story

Dodo Island is rich in various natural resources. It began with only two types of EtherEagles initially. After suffering several animal mass extinctions, a new group came. Due to the relationship with humans, two new groups were added. But recently, the zombie virus outbreak is continuing to grow. It comes a new group. Now, here are a total of 9 groups. The fire area which is abundant with lava and geothermal spring is located on the west of Dodo Island. Some certain EtherEagles often rest here. The grass area can be called the land of plenty. The forest hums with life. It is easy to find the EtherEagles sing sweetly here. The water area is located in the eastern of Dodo Island which was a prosperous river civilization.

About

Hi! We are a team from Taiwan with highly motivated members building an appealing and elaborate collection. We’ve been working together for quite a while. We are improving and creating the right chemistry now. The very early number from EtherEagle # 1 to #96 are meticulously designed and handcrafted. Starting from EtherEagle #97- #4096 are automatic system generation, each is one of a kind and exhibit featuring accessories. Surprisingly, there are 100 works among EtherEagle #97- #4096 randomly plus team members’ inspiration. Also, you could view the radar chart of each work, with an eye to learning more characteristics. We hope you’ll like our collection.
